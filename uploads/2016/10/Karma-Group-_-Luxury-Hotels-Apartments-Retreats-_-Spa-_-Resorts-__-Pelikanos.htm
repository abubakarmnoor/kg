    Karma Pelikanos, Mykonos

    The gleaming whitewashed walls of Karma Pelikanos are located at the most beautiful location on the Greek island of Mykonos. 

    This standalone residence is the newest addition to the Karma portfolio; an exclusive hideaway that is quietly self-contained yet still compellingly close to the island’s fabled beaches and hedonistic nightlife. The stunning design combines traditional architecture with 21st century amenities, including an infinity pool with 180° degree views of the azure Aegean Sea. Enjoy barefoot luxury in the playground paradise of Pelikanos.

    Location

    For Millennium, Mykonos and it’s sister island, Delos, have been at the centre of Greek society and civilization. Today, its visitors include celebrities and the international elite, who come for the island’s unique mix of cuisine, scenery, culture, stunning natural beauty, restaurants and clubs.

    Karma Pelikanos is situated only five-minutes from the airport and 10-minutes from Mykonos town, between Paradise and Super Paradise beaches.

    How to get here

    Arrive by plane or ferry and you will be transported directly to Karma Pelikanos.

    What’s near

    The restaurants and tavernas of Paradise, Super Paradise and many other beaches are minutes away by foot or car.

    Wander the maze of ancient streets and browse exclusive designer boutiques in Mykonos Town; tour the island’s picturesque countryside; or visit the island’s only inland settlement, the village of Ano Mera, for traditional culture.

    Amenities

    Amenities

    Karma Pelikanos is stand alone luxury villa that celebrates the old world charm of traditional Greek building design, combined with amenities and interior design of the 21st century, including a large pool and veranda for entertaining, complete with barbeque and pizza oven. Casting a 180-degree gaze westward across the sparkling waters of the Aegean, a stay at our estate will be remarkable.

    • Private concierge

      Our concierge knows who to call to secure guest list entry to the best clubs, event tickets and restaurant reservations. If you want to hire a moped to explore the island or a yacht to sail its seas, we will make it happen.

    • Professional chef

      Using locally sourced, seasonal ingredients, a private chef will prepare meals for you, on request. Enjoy Greek tapas, fresh organic salads and sumptuous Mediterranean feasts, complemented by fine world wines and fiery local spirits.

    • Heated infinity pool

      Appearing to recede into the Aegean waters, the heated infinity pool is a slice of blue paradise in the estate’s gardens. Surrounded by a large terrace with a hedonistic bamboo-shaded lounge area, we predict this will be your social hub and the place to host the ultimate private party.

    • Villa Transport

      Karma Pelikanos comes equipped with 2 vehicles – a Wrangler Jeep and a small car which are an ideal way to explore the narrow roads of Mykonos. It’s the ideal way to reach the secluded beaches and off-the-radar bars and restaurants that are the island’s best-kept secrets.

    • Wifi

      In our connected world it’s vital to stay in touch, however dreamy your destination. High-speed wifi and broadband access allows you to dip into the real world whenever you need.

      International satellite TV

      All the on-screen news and entertainment you expect, plus a library of films and programming to explore at your leisure.

       

    • Childcare service

      Make the most of Mykonos’s hottest beach clubs, secure in the knowledge that your children are safely entertained by our professional in-house team. Registered and fully vetted childcare service is available on request.

    • Karma Concierge Shopping Service

      Coordinated by our private concierge and housekeeper. Simply tell us what requirements you have for food, drink, pool inflatables, etc and we will ensure they are stocked on your arrival.

    Accommodation

    Accommodation

    Perfect for a family gathering or a party of friends, Karma Pelikanos comprises three individual villas sleeping 16 guests in total. The accommodation is designed to flow seamlessly for inside-outside living. Airy interiors featuring marble, wood and designer fabrics open on to a stunning alfresco lounge area with comfortable banquettes beneath a bamboo pergola. There is an outdoor barbecue and pizza oven, plus heated infinity pool. Each bedroom also has a private terrace for quiet moments.
